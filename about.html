About us

Abdullah Al-Sehati Factory for Plastic Industries, better known as SAIPIN

Abdullah Al-Saihati Factory for Plastic Industries, commonly known as SAIPIN is a manufacturing company of the Al-Saihati Group of Companies established in 2003G, engaged in the manufacture of plastic products including plastic files, specialty folders and office stationery, with a product range of more than 500 items.

SAIPIN annually converts 1,000 tons of raw materials consisting of PVC, paper cards, Rexene, Ring mechanism, paper and other accessories to manufacture one million end products of a variety. Over a period of time, SAIPIN has designed and developed a large number of industrial and consumer products and import alternatives that meet the regular needs of various customers. The detailed product portfolio is laid out for a quick overview.

SAIPIN has attained the required ISO 9001:2015 certification, and the in-house quality control facilities developed by SAIPIN are the best in the industry, in full compliance with national and international standards, extending unrivaled quality guarantees to its valued customers. A team of hardworking and loyal employees is the backbone of the company, as they continue to constantly innovate SAIPIN product bags facilitating round the clock customer service across the Kingdom.
